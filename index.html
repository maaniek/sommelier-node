Questions,
answered.

Does Sommelier Node fit any wine bottle?

Yes. The clip fits any standard wine bottle neck, still or sparkling, with no adapters required.

How long does the battery last?

A single charge lasts a full day of service — engineered by Vorwerk for all-day restaurant and winery use.

Does it work with sparkling wine and other beverages?

Yes. Sommelier Node reads temperature and aeration for still wine, sparkling wine, and other bottled beverages.

How does the app connect to the device?

Over Bluetooth. The app shows live temperature and aeration status, and can monitor multiple devices at once.

Is it suitable for restaurants and wineries, not just home use?

Yes. Restaurants and wineries can monitor multiple bottles and tables from a single dashboard in the app.

What's included, and what's the warranty?

Each Sommelier Node ships with the device, a charging dock, and access to the companion app — covered by Vorwerk's standard product warranty.
